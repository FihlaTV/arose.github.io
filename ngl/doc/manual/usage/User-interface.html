There are four major elements in the GUI: the *Menubar* at the top; the *Statusbar* at the bottom; the *Sidebar* on the left side; and the *Viewport* in the center of the screen.

Menubar

  • File menu
  • View menu
  • Examples menu
  • Help menu

Statusbar

Picking information is shown on the left side.

Sidebar

The sidebar hosts an interface element for each loaded structure and added representation. A number of buttons are available to quickly access commands:
  • hide/show (eye icon)
  • center (bulls eye icon)
  • delete (trash bin icon)
  • parameters menu (stacked bars icon)

Selection inputs

Input fields for atom selections (funnel icon) restrict the display of representations.
A selection input is a text field in which to enter [page:Selection-language selection strings]. It grows or shrinks to adjust to the selection length. Upon typing the background is colored blue to indicate changes that are not applied. Pressing the *Enter* key applies the selection and the background is colored red or white to report whether the selection is incorrect or correct, respectively.

Viewport

The viewport at the center is the canvas where molecular visualizations are rendered to.

Mouse controls

  • Left button hold and move: rotate camera around center.
  • Left button click: pick atom.
  • Middle button hold and move: zoom camera in and out.
  • Middle button click: center camera on atom.
  • Right button hold and move: translate camera in screen plane.
